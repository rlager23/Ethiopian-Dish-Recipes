Awaze tibs

Awaze Tibs is a mouthwatering Ethipian dish featuring tender pieces of meat, often beef, cooked with onion, tomato paste,peppers, spices and a special sauce called "awaze." The meat and sauce are cooked together until tender, resulting in flavorful and aromatic dish.

Beyaynetu

Beyaynetu is a popular dish that offers vibrant and diverse array of textures. It's a vegetarian platter, consisting of variety of dishes served together with selection of stews, salads, and sides. It's served with Injera, Ethiopian flatbread made from teff flour.

kilkil

Kilkil is a tradional Ethiopian dish. It's a colorful and hearty stew. Typically made with a variety of vegetables and aromatic spices. It's not only delicious but also nutritious. Ingredients include, 2 cup green beans, 1cup carrots, sliced, 2medium potatoes, 1 onion, 2 cloves garlic minced, 2 table spoon oil, 1 teaspoon turmeric, 1 teaspoon paprika, salt and water.

Doro Wot

Doro Wat, also known as Ethiopian Spicy Chicken Stew, is a flavorful and aromatic dish that’s a staple in Ethiopian cuisine. It’s rich, spicy, and absolutely delicious. Ingredients included, 2 lbs chicken (legs and thighs, bone-in) 2 large onions, finely chopped 4 cloves of garlic, minced 1-inch piece of fresh ginger, grated 1/4 to 1/3 cup berbere spice blend (adjust to the preferred level of spiciness) 1/4 cup vegetable oil 1 tsp paprika Salt, to taste Water
